Chicken Fritters

The must amazing chicken friiters

This is the most moist and delicious chicken patties ever. Can also use turkey if chicken is not your style.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken thighs
  • italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • onions
  • egg
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • Vegtable Oil

Directions

  1. Mix chicken, bread crumbs, onion, mozzarella cheese, egg, mayonnaise, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a bowl until well combined. Shape mixture into 8 equal-sized patties.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet. Fry patties until lightly browned, about 5 minutes. Flip and fry for an additional 3 to 5 minutes. Transfer fritters to a paper towel-lined plate.

Cook's Notes:

I used chicken thighs because I think they are more flavorful but chicken breasts will work.

I use Walmart's brand of chicken dipping sauce which is very similar to the one from that famous chicken restaurant.
